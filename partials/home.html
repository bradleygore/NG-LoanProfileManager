Loan Analytics

by Bradley Gore

Welcome to Loan Analytics, a place where you can see how much you can get out of your loan in addition to how much your loan is getting out of you!

Our application utilizes your own local storage to hold the Loan Profiles you create and is thusly fully compatible with the following browsers:

  • Internet Explorer 8+
  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
However, even if your browser doesn't support local storage, you can create and modify as many Loan Profiles as you like during your browsing session!
*Note: Whichever browser you create your profiles in will be the browser that can access the profiles later on. Local Storage is unique within each browser.

Loan Analytics provides a fully featured experience for analyzing your loans. Here's what we have to offer:

  • Create multiple unique Loan Profiles
  • Continously update Loan Profiles with
    • Single-Instance Extra Payments on any month in your payment schedule
    • Recurring Extra Payments that span multiple months and/or years in your payment schedule
    • General Scenario modifications
  • Remove Loan Profiles as you're done with them
